From Practitioner to Architect: The Mindset Shift

Welcome to your interactive study plan for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03) exam. This guide is designed for professionals like you who have hands-on experience with AWS services but want to bridge the gap to architectural thinking. The key to success is shifting your focus from "How do I use this service?" to "Why was this service chosen, and how does it create a secure, resilient, high-performing, and cost-optimized system?"

This entire plan is built around the AWS Well-Architected Framework, which is the conceptual compass for the exam and the architect role. Use this dashboard to navigate through the key areas of study, from understanding the exam structure to hands-on labs that will solidify your knowledge.
